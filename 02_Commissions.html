    Ten Years of Reading

  • Data in gouache on mirror
  • 6 ft x 5 ft x 4 in.
  • 2021 - 2023

by Ninna Gaensler-Debs & Cal Tabuena-Frolli

Installed in the private library of NGD and Patrick Galvin by CTF and Camille Wong in 2023, San Francisco.

Photographs by Jessie Badrous

Since 2011, my friend Ninna has kept a log of the books she's read.

This physical documentary in painted glass is a reflection of her list as of 2021, 472 books.

Each mirror represents an author; colors represent genre

During the production of this piece, I made 5 material studies ( Friend Pieces ) and a book of auxiliary research called Only the Gold 01
